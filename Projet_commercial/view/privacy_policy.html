JDC Services business policy:

Our company is dedicated to being an integral part of our youth in the working world. Our slogan "Live with a light heart" represents the relief of your worries. Thanks to our motivated, conscious and dynamic young people, we are able to offer you support and assistance for any type of need in your daily life. JDC Services is aware of the many difficulties that are present in the life of an elderly person. Our team is aware of the abilities of people in the 15-25 age group, which is why we work towards their professional integration. We work together with the Mission Locale Sémaphore and the Pôle Emploi du Haut-Rhin. Our motivation over the years comes from the fact that having noticed various forms of mistreatment of our seniors, we could no longer tolerate it. We therefore had the idea to develop our company based on various services to the person. Translated with www.DeepL.com/Translator (free version)
