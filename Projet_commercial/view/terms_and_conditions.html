General terms and conditions of sale

The pre-contractual information is organized by the II of the article L. 441-1 of the Commercial code which makes obligation with any person exerting activities of production, distribution or services which establishes general conditions of sale to communicate them to any purchaser who makes the request for a professional activity. This communication is made by any means constituting a durable medium. The general terms and conditions of sale may be differentiated according to the categories of purchasers of products or applicants for services (e.g. retailers, wholesalers). In this case, the communication obligation only applies to purchasers of products or applicants for services of the same category. As the general terms and conditions of sale constitute the sole basis for commercial negotiation, any producer, service provider, wholesaler or importer may, moreover, within the framework of this negotiation, agree with a purchaser of products or a person requesting the provision of services on specific terms and conditions of sale which are not subject to this obligation to communicate. Pursuant to IV of Article L. 441-1 of the French Commercial Code, the refusal to communicate existing general terms and conditions of sale to any buyer who requests them for a professional activity is subject to an administrative fine of up to €15,000 for a natural person and €75,000 for a legal person.
